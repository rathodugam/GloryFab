Product Description

Since our inception, we have been committed to offering elegantly designed Poly Vinyle Fabric. Offered fabric is designed using superior quality yarns and threads which are sourced from reliable market vendors. This fabric is designed in various styles, patterns as well as sizes as per clients' choice. It is widely used in making of bags, mattresses as well as upholstery. Poly Vinyle Fabric is demanded due to its resistant to creasing, color fastness as well as waterproof properties.

Features:

  • Fade resistance
  • Soft fabric
  • Long lasting
