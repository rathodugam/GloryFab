Company Profile

Set-up our business in the textile capital of India, Surat, we, Glory Fab LLP. have in-house facility for weaving, dyeing, knitting, raising, shearing and lamination processes. Employed with proficient team of technical staff members, computer engineers, textile specialists and administrative professionals, each employee hold expertise in their respective fields and have standardized working system. To make sure that only premium range of Office Chair Fabric, Ortho Fabric, Shoes Fabric, Bags & Luggage Fabrics, etc., are offered to the customers, we are continuously focusing on the factors like business expansion, improvement in the existing product's quality and incorporation of latest technological innovations.
